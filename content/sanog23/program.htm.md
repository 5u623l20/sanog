SANOG XXIII  
13-21 January 2014, Thimphu, Bhutan

The venue for SANOG XXIII is Thimphu, Bhutan. The event will be hosted
by Bhutan Telecom. SANOG 23 will consists of Workshops, Tutorials and
Conferences spread over 9 Days.
