<td><p>SANOG XXII<br />
5-13 August, 2013, Mumbai, India<br />
<em><strong>10 Years of Networking Excellence in South Asia</strong></em></p>
<p>The venue for SANOG XXII is Mumbai, India. The meeting is hosted by the ISP Association of India.</p>
<p>Mumbai is the largest city in India. It's also the business capital of India, as well as state capital of the state of Maharastra. Mumbai, previoulsy known as Bombay, is an important sea port and commercial center in the Arabian Sea/ Indian Ocean region.</p>
<p>More details about Mumbai are on these sites.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.mumbainet.com/">Mumbai on the net</a></p>
