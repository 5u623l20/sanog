<td><p>SANOG VI<br />
16-23 July, 2005, Thimphu Bhutan<br />
</p>
<p>The venue for SANOG VI is Thimphu, Bhutan.</p>
<p><img src="images/banquethall.jpg" width="300" height="119" /></p>
<p>All the workshops, tutorials and conference will be hosted at the Royal Banquet Hall.</p>
<p><strong>About Thimpu</strong></p>
<p>Thimphu is located at an altitude of about 2300 meters above sea level. Though most visitors do not have a problem adjusting to the high altitude, however if you are not used to it, then you might want to take the necessary steps.</p>
<p>Although SANOG is going to be held during the summer season, it should be noted that during windy and rainy days, the temperature can be as low as 15 C.</p></td>
