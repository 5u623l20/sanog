23-30 July, 2004 Kathmandu, Nepal</p>
<p><strong>West Asia BoF</strong><br />
</p>
<p>The West Asia BoF is the result of the agreed co-operation between the RIPE NCC and SANOG. SANOG will encourage participation from the West Asia (Middle East) region and RIPE will provide the support and services to the participants from those regions. The Chair and co-chair of this BoF are the Chair and co-chair of the RIPE Member Services Working group.</p>
<p><em>Chair: Kurt Erik Lindqvist<br />
Co-chair: Bijal Sanghani</em></p>
