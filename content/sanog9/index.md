<p><strong>SANOG 9 Keynotes :</strong></p>
<p><strong><a href="keynote1.htm">Network Design and Planning: An Art or a Science?</a></strong><br />
<a href="speakers.htm">Dr. Vishal Sharma, Metonia Inc.</a></p>
<p>This keynote will look at different approaches at managing and planning for network development and deployment.</p>
<p>Future of Service Provider's Network in South Asia.<br />
Srinivas Mulugu</p>
<p>This keynote will highlight the experience of the speaker from working with some of the largest ISPs in the region, and also outside the region.</p>
<p><strong>About SANOG IX</strong></p>
<p>SANOG IX will be hosted by Sri Lanka Telecom in Colombo, Sri Lanka. SANOG 9 will also be sponsored mainly by SLT. SANOG is being hosted for the first time in Sri-Lanka in its current form with workshop, tutorials and the conference. SANOG 9 will also have an another approach to the SANOG program, with a two day gap between the workshops and the rest of the event, to give the workshop attendees two days to re-cover from the hectic workshop schedule.</p></td>
