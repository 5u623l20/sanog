<td><p>SANOG 8<br />
27 July - 4 August 2006, Karachi, Pakistan</p>
<p><span class="style1"><span class="style2 style3"><span class="style4">Visa Information</span><br />
<br />
All foreign nationals, except for those belonging to countries with which Pakistan has Visa Abolition Agreements, information availible with Pakistan Missions abroad, are required to obtain a visa to enter Pakistan. Visa can be obtained from Pakistan's Diplomatic Missions. Ordinarily Missions deal only with visa applicants residing in thier jurisdiction he/she resides at the time of making application, will deal with the case. </span></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.pakistan.gov.pk/foreignaffairs-ministry/informationandservices/visa/visa.jsp">Details available at Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Visa page</a></p>
<p>NSP will issue invitation letters to those who'll need an invitation letter for visa applications. Please forward your registration e-mail to sanog@nsp.org.pk when requesting the invitation. Or write to sanog@nsp.org.pk with your details including Full name, Organization Name, Address and passport number.</p>
