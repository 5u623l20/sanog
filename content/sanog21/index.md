<td><p>SANOG XXI<br />
27 January -4 February, 2013, Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh<br />
<em><strong>10 Years of Networking Excellence in South Asia</strong></em></p>
<p>The venue for SANOG XXI is Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh. The meeting would be hosted by the ISP Association of Bangladesh.</p>
<p>March 10, 2013: <a href="downloads.htm">SANOG 21 Downloads Available !</a><a href="downloads.htm"></a></p>
<p>February 10, 2013: <a href="http://www.sanog.org/sanog21/gallery">SANOG 21 Picture Gallery Posted!</a><a href="http://www.sanog.org/sanog21/gallery"></a></p>
<p>Janurary 27, 2013: <a href="https://apnic.adobeconnect.com/sanog21">SANOG 21 LIVE: January 27 - January 28, 2013, Watche LIVE Now!</a><a href="https://apnic.adobeconnect.com/sanog21"></a></p>
<p>December 25, 2012: <a href="fellowship.htm">Fellowship Results are Published</a></p>
<p>December 25, 2012: <a href="registration.htm">Registration is Open</a></p>
<p>December 15, 2012: <a href="accommodation.htm">Accommodation Booking and Domestic Flight booking</a></p>
<p>December 15, 2012: Paul Wilson, DG, APNIC confirmed as Keynote Speaker</p>
<p>November 5, 2012: <a href="cfp.htm">Call for Papers</a></p>
<p>November 3, 2012: <a href="program.htm">Draft Agenda is published</a></p>
<p>November 1, 2012 : <a href="fellowship.htm">SANOG Fellowship is open</a></p>
<p>NOTE: SANOG XXI will follow a different structure with the conferrence opening the event, followed by tutorials and finally workshops.</p>
<p><strong>'Internet Governance: why Operators should care" Keynote Address by Paul Wilson, DG, APNIC</strong></p>
<p>Paul Wilson, Director General, APNIC, will give a Keynote speech on the importance of the ongoing Internet Governance issues for Operators. Paul Wilson has been an active participant in the Internet Governance issues since the early WSIS prepcoms, and will bring insights to opertors on why it's important to care about it.</p>
<p>About SANOG XXI</p>
<p>With the 21st edition, SANOG celeberates 10 full years of SANOG. SANOG was initiated in January 2003, and in 2013, it has completed full 10 years. This is the fourth time that SANOG is coming to Bangladesh, and so to a different city then Dhaka. Cox's Bazar is the best known tourist destination of Bangladesh, and boasts of the longest beach in the region. It will also take participants away from the traffic and hustle of Dhaka.<br />
</p>
<p>SANOG XXI is hosted by ISP Association of Banbladesh, with support from various different local agencies and sponros.</p>
