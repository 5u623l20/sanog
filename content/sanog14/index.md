<strong>Keynote Speaker : Ram Mohan, Afilias</strong><br />
<span class="style1 style2 style3">Ram is a recognized expert[1] in Internationalized Domain Names (IDNs)[2] the effort to make domain names work in scripts and languages other than ASCII/English[3]. He was a co-author of the IDN Guidelines that have been widely adopted by registries worldwide, and has authored, edited or reviewed the majority of ICANN IDN technical and policy documents. He is a member of the IDN President's Advisory Committee. He chaired the ICANN Working Group on IDNs. He was an invited expert to the ICANN Reserved Names Working Group. He now chairs the Security and Stability Advisory Committee SSAC IDN Study Group. </span><br />

<p>The venue for SANOG 14 is Chennai, India. Chennai was previously known as Madras. The ISP Association of India (ISPAI) will host the event once again.</p>
<p>ISPAI and SANOG are glad to be back in India after the combined SANOG10/APNIC24 in New Delhi.</p>
<p>Chennai is a unique location in South India, and is also a major fiber landing station, with lots of national and international ISPs with presence in this metro.<br />
</p>
