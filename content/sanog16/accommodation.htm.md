<td><p>SANOG XVI<br />
15-23 July, 2010 Paro, Bhutan</p>
<p>The main accommodation for SANOG XVI has been arranged at the Renchin Ling Lodge in Paro. This hotel is about 15-20 minutes walk from the conference venue.</p>
<p>Room Rates:</p>
<p>Single Delux Room : Nu 1700.00<br />
Twin Sharing (single Occupancy) : Nu 1900.00<br />
Twin Sharing (Double Occupancy): Nu 1400.00 per night per person</p>
<p>Rate includes all Taxes. Breakfast is not included and is Nu. 150/per person.</p>
<p>Please use this <a href="https://submission.sanog.org/hotel/">ONLINE FORM</a> to book accommodation, you'll get an confirmation from the SANOG 16 hosts.</p>
<p>Accommodation will be booked by the hosts, as part of the visa process. If you are from India or Bangladesh and/or do not need visas in advance, please use the online form as well.</p>
